Bio

I am a recent graduate from the University of Michigan with a bachelor's degree in astronomy & astrophysics/physics. Currently, I am working with Professor Yuan Li at the University of North Texas as a bridge fellow in the TEXAS Bridge Program.

Outside of my research interests (you can find those under the Research tab) I am a big fan of bugs! I got involved with the Entomolgy Club at UMich and became the Outreach Chair after seeing so many cool bugs while I was staying at KPNO.

I also am just a big fan of the outdoors in general. I love hiking, camping, snowboarding, and rock climbing.
